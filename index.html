Safer Construction Through Better Visibility

BLEprint helps construction teams see what is behind walls by turning build-time progress photos into an AR overlay for later use on site.

Accidents and utility strikes happen when workers do not know what is hidden behind walls. BLEprint is building a software-first solution that lets users upload progress photos during construction and later use those records to visualize hidden infrastructure before drilling, cutting, or renovating.
