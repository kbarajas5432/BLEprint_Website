What BLEprint changes

From passive site photos to a usable field reference.

Construction teams already document progress. BLEprint is built to turn that documentation into a practical system for understanding hidden infrastructure later in the job lifecycle.

Learn the full story
Before BLEprint

Photos sit in folders and context gets lost.

Crews return to finished walls with fragmented knowledge and limited confidence in what is hidden behind the surface.

With BLEprint

The record stays tied to the wall, not buried in storage.

Images are organized by location and timing so teams can reference hidden systems with more confidence before the work starts.
